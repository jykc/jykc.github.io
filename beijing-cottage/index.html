Welcome to Beijing Cottage


Located in the South End of Croydon High Street, we specialise in traditional authentic Cantonese and Peking cuisines for over 20 years.

With a variety of dishes which can bring delight to your palate from our classic aromatic duck to the finest range of seafood dishes: lobsters, mussels, scallops, soft shell crabs, fishes, king prawns and our speciality sizzling dishes served on a very hot plate. So come in and enjoy and let us serve you our tasty dishes!
